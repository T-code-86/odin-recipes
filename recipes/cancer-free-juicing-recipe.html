"Cancer-Fighting" Juicing Recipes

Description

The protective effect of different plant-based foods against cancer is well proven, both in laboratory studies and epidemiological studies in humans. The anti-cancer cure makes it possible to consume these foods in the form of beverages. (Healthy Juices, Dr. George D. Pamplona-Roger)

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup of Broccoli
  • 1 Cup of Cucumber
  • 2 Cups of Romaine Lettuce
  • 1/2 Cup of Cilantro
  • 1/2 Green Apple * Optional
  • 1 Lime

Steps

  1. Thoroughly Wash each ingredient
  2. Rest each into a clean container
  3. Pass each through the juicing machine
