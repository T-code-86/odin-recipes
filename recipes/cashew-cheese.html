Cashew Cheese Recipe

Description

This recipe was first given to me by my sister Kerly-Ann. She is an amazaing cook. Cashew cheese is a natural alternative to dairy cheeses.It is delicious to most people. It's a great cheese paste for the lactose intolerant and tolerant.

Ingredients

  • Raw Cashew Nuts
  • 2-3 pegs of garlic
  • Red flavour pepper
  • 2 Dashes of lemon/lime juice
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Place all of the ingredients into a blender
  2. Add water to the blender to reach the level of cashew nuts
  3. Blend all of the ingredients in a blender
  4. Place the smoothly blended mixture into a sauce pan
  5. Stir over low heat until desired consistency is reached
  6. Store in a glass container and refrigerate once cool
