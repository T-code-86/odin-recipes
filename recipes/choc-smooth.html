Chocolate Hazelnut Smoothie Recipe

Description

This Chocolate Hazelnut Smoothie is rich in Vitamin E. The Vitamin E is good for your skin and hair. You can toss some greens in here and easily mask the flavour with the rich tones of hazelnut, chocolate, and hemp seeds. It would be great for breakfast.

Ingredients for Hazelnut Milk

  • 1 cup hazelnuts
  • 3-4 cups of water
  • 1/2 tsp of vanilla bean powder
  • 1/8 tsp of himalayan or sea salt

Steps

  1. Soak 1 cup of hazelnuts in water overnight
  2. Rinse soaked hazelnuts off thoroughly
  3. Blend with 3-4 cups of water and vanilla bean
  4. Filter through a mesh nut milk bag and store in a glass far for 3-4 days in the fridge
  5. Add 1-2 tsp of maple syrup or honey as optional sweeteners.

Ingredients for Chocolate Hazelnut Smoothie

  • 1/2 avocado
  • 1/2 banana
  • 2 tbsp cacao
  • 1 tsp maca powder -optional-
  • 1 tbsp hemp seeds or hemp oil -or both-
  • 1-2 cups hazelnut milk -which was made earlier-
  • 1 cup spinach or leafy green -optional-
  • 4 blocks of ice cubes -optional-
  1. Blend your ingredients together until smooth
  2. Serve chilled
